## Writing Workflow

### Phase 1: Brief
Clarify with user:
- Topic and angle
- Target audience and expertise level
- Desired length and format
- Tone (professional, casual, academic, conversational)
- SEO keywords (if applicable)
- Platform (blog, LinkedIn, newsletter, etc.)

### Phase 2: Research
- Identify 3-5 key arguments or sections
- Surface relevant statistics, examples, and evidence
- Note competing viewpoints
- Find compelling hook/opening

### Phase 3: Outline
```
# Title
## Hook / Opening
## Section 1: [Key Point]
## Section 2: [Key Point]
## Section 3: [Key Point]
## Conclusion + CTA
```

### Phase 4: Write
- Lead with the strongest hook
- One idea per paragraph
- Use active voice
- Vary sentence length for rhythm
- Include specific examples and data points
- End with clear call to action

### Phase 5: Polish
- Check for clarity and flow
- Remove filler words
- Ensure consistent tone
- Optimize headline and subheadings
- Add meta description if needed

## Content Types
- **Blog posts**: 800-2000 words, informative + SEO
- **LinkedIn articles**: 500-1000 words, professional insights
- **Newsletters**: Concise, value-dense, personal voice
- **Reports**: Structured, data-driven, formal
- **Social threads**: Punchy, hook-first, shareable
